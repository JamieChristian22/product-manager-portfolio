## Overview
This case study demonstrates how product analytics, user research, and experimentation were used to increase adoption of a newly launched feature within a digital product environment. The project simulates a real-world Product Manager workflow from discovery to outcome measurement.

## Problem Statement
A recently released feature showed **low engagement and poor visibility**, despite strong development investment. Users were either unaware of the feature or did not understand its value, leading to underutilization and missed revenue/retention opportunities.

- Demonstrate measurable business impact
