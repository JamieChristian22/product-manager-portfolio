# Revenue & Pricing Strategy

## Project Overview
This case study focuses on optimizing revenue growth and pricing strategy using data analysis, financial modeling, and product experimentation.  
The goal is to identify opportunities for pricing tier restructuring and forecast the financial impact of pricing decisions on key business metrics.

---

## Problem Statement
Despite growing traffic and engagement, revenue growth was flatter than expected.  
We seek to analyze pricing effectiveness, model elasticity, recommend strategic price tiers, and forecast revenue impact.

---

## Key Objectives
- Assess pricing elasticity across customer segments  
- Improve Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)  
- Increase upgrade rates across subscription tiers  
- Forecast revenue impact based on pricing adjustments  
- Communicate strategic recommendations clearly to stakeholders
