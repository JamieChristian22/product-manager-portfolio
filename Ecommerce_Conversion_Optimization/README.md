# Ecommerce Conversion Optimization

## Project Overview
This case study focuses on identifying and resolving drop-offs in an ecommerce checkout funnel to increase conversion rates and revenue. It demonstrates end-to-end product management work — from analytics and hypothesis validation to product decisions and impact measurement.

---

## Problem Statement
Users frequently abandon the checkout process before completing purchases, resulting in lost revenue. The goal was to analyze funnel behavior, identify friction points, and design product improvements to increase completed transactions.
